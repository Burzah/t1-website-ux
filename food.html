Dine Taniti!

Food and more!

Whether you're craving fresh island flavors, familiar comfort food, or quick groceries for your stay, Taniti has options to satisfy every appetite. From seafood by the bay to late night convenience stores, eating well is easy on the island.

  • Restaurants:
    • The Driftwood Grill – local fish and rice
    • Bay Breeze Café – casual island fusion
    • Seaside Catch – waterfront seafood dining
    • Ocean Ember – rustic local flavors
    • Rainforest Table – farm-to-table island cuisine
    • Island Diner – American-style comfort food
    • The Taniti Griddle – pancakes, burgers, fries
    • North Shore Eats – fast-casual American
    • Lotus Grove – Pan-Asian noodles and rice bowls
    • Sakura Sands – sushi and tropical stir-fry
  • Grocery Stores:
    • Taniti Market – full-service supermarket
    • Harbor Fresh Foods – supermarket near the bay
    • Koko's Corner Store – smaller neighborhood grocer
    • Jungle Basket – local produce and dry goods
    • SunUp Stop – 24-hour convenience store
