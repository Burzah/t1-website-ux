Explore Taniti!

Activities

Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or nightlife, Taniti has something for everyone. From outdoor thrills to cultural experiences, the island is packed with exciting ways to spend your days and nights.

  • Snorkeling and scuba diving
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Rainforest zip-lining
  • Nightlife including pubs, a microbrewery, and a new dance club
  • Local history museum
  • Helicopter rides
  • Art galleries and live music venues
  • Movie theater, arcade, and bowling alley
  • Upcoming 9-hole golf course (opening soon!)

Sightseeing

Taniti is a photographer’s dream and an explorer’s playground. From lush rainforests to ancient architecture, the island’s natural and cultural highlights offer unforgettable views and experiences.

  • White-sand beaches surrounding Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Hiking trails through tropical rainforests
  • Guided tours to the active volcano
  • Boat and bus tours around the island
  • Taniti City’s native architecture and markets
  • Scenic overlooks near Merriton Landing
