Welcome to Taniti!

Paradise on the Sea

Welcome to Taniti, a hidden gem in the Pacific that blends beauty, culture, and adventure. Though small, Taniti offers an incredible variety of experiences. From relaxing on soft, white-sand beaches to exploring lush rainforests and even hiking up an active volcano. Whether you’re snorkeling in crystal clear waters, zip lining through the jungle canopy, enjoying fresh seafood by the bay, or dancing the night away in a tropical club, Taniti has something for every traveler. Stay in everything from cozy B&Bs to luxury resorts and even hostels. Explore charming Taniti City or the vibrant, growing hub of Merriton Landing. With convenient transportation, friendly locals, and a warm island atmosphere, Taniti is more than a destination, it’s an experience waiting to be discovered.
