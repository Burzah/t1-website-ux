Getting Around Taniti

Exploring Taniti is easy and convenient. Public buses operate in Taniti City daily from 5:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m., while private buses cover more rural areas. Taxis and rental cars are also available. Many visitors even enjoy renting bikes to explore at a relaxed pace. Taniti City is flat and walkable, especially around the lively Merriton Landing area.
